#BlackStar

<font color=#0099ff>BlackStar</font> is a general APS (advanced planning & scheduling) model and algorithm solution for various industries including aerospace mission, unmanned systems and vehicle fleets. BlackStar uses a continuous time model for precision events, supporting Unified Scheduling Language (USL), rule mechanism and drag-n-drop operations in Gantt Charts to simplify applications in industries.
